About

TheBenn is a passionate music producer blending dark, immersive beats with modern edge. Hailing from Slovenia and evolving with the times, he embraces technology and creativity to produce a sound that is both innovative and heartfelt.

As a multi-talented artist, TheBenn crafts beats, creates mood-enhancing soundscapes, and pushes artistic boundaries — all while connecting with audiences in real time.

"If you can feel the rhythm, then you know the passion behind every track."
